 <p><b>Reductionism</b> is a disbelief that the higher levels of simplified multilevel models are out there in the <a href="/wiki/Territory" title="Territory" class="mw-redirect">territory</a>, that concepts constructed by mind in themselves play a role in the behavior of reality. This doesn't contradict the notion that the concepts used in simplified multilevel models refer to the actual clusters of configurations of reality. </p>
